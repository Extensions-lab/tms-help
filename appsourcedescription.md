# AppSource Product Description

Use this page as the short product positioning source for the help site, AppSource text, and partner handover material.

TMS for Logistics Service Providers helps freight forwarders, brokers, and 3PL teams manage multi-stage forwarding, carrier execution, settlement, and customer invoicing in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

## Who it is for

Use TMS when your company manages transportation for customers or other parties and must control both operations and finance.

Best fit:

- freight forwarders,
- logistics service providers,
- brokers,
- 3PL teams,
- Business Central partners implementing forwarding workflows.

## What the app helps users do

1. Capture the customer request in a [Forwarding Order](forwardingorder.md).
2. Split the movement into [Stages](stages.md).
3. Execute stages with [Freight Orders](freightorder.md).
4. Allocate carrier costs in [Settlement](settlement.md).
5. Create customer invoices and credit memos.
6. Monitor income, cost, profit, and uninvoiced work.

## Supported Business Central editions

TMS supports the Essential and Premium editions of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

## Supported countries and languages

TMS is intended for countries and regions where Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is available.

The public listing states that the app is available in English, German, Spanish, Turkish, and Dutch.
